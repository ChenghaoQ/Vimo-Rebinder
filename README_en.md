### **Vimo Rebinder: Master Shortcuts, Boost Your Productivity**

[中文版本](README_zh.md) | [English Version](README.md)

**Vimo Rebinder** helps you efficiently master a large number of shortcuts, boosting your productivity while keeping you focused. By reducing reliance on the mouse, it enables faster workflows and supports single-handed operation for complex tasks.

---

### **Key Features**

* **Save Time**: Minimize mouse usage and stay focused on the keyboard.
* **Easy Memorization**: Memorize over 50 shortcuts with just 5-8 keystrokes.
* **Single-Handed Operation**: Optimized layout to perform complex tasks with one hand.
* **Cross-Platform**: Use unified shortcuts across Windows, Mac, and multiple applications.
* **Repeat Action**: Quickly execute the last-used shortcut with ease.
* **Customizable Global Shortcuts**: Supports **unlimited shortcuts**.
* **Action Customization**: Tailor shortcuts to fit your operational needs and boost productivity.
* **Lightweight & Efficient**: Runs smoothly without consuming excessive system resources.

---


### **Installation Instructions**

1. Download and extract the Vimo Rebinder zip file.
2. For **Windows**: Double-click `Vimo Rebinder.exe` to run the app.
   For **MacOS**: Download directly from the **Mac App Store**.
3. Follow the installation wizard to complete the setup.
4. Launch the application, configure your settings, and start using custom shortcuts.

---
### **Usage Instructions**

1. Open **Vimo Rebinder** and access the settings interface.
2. Click "Add Shortcut" to assign keys and actions.
3. Click "Save" to apply the configuration.
4. You can edit or delete any shortcuts at any time, with changes taking effect immediately.
